About 72jv

72jv is the official URL shortening service created by Maxenoz Mega. It provides short, easy-to-share links that redirect to websites, apps, documents, videos, and other online content.

Why use 72jv?

  • Short and memorable links
  • Easy to share on social media
  • Official links from Maxenoz projects
  • Custom short links for approved clients
  • Simple and fast redirects

How do I know a link is official?

If a link starts with https://72jv.github.io/..., it is an official 72jv short link managed by Maxenoz Mega.

Become a client

Businesses, creators, and organizations may request their own custom 72jv short link. Once approved, you'll receive a memorable URL that redirects visitors to your website or project.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is 72jv free?
Official Maxenoz links are free for Maxenoz. Client links may have different requirements depending on the project.

Can anyone create links?
Currently, new short links are created and managed by Maxenoz Mega.

Do links expire?
No. Official 72jv links are intended to remain available for the long term.
